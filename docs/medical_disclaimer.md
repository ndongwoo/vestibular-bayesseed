# Medical disclaimer

Vestibular-BayesSeed is intended for research, education, software demonstration, and methodological development only.

It is not a validated clinical decision-support system. It is not a medical device. It must not be used for clinical diagnosis, treatment decisions, triage, patient counseling, or patient management.

## Coefficient disclaimer

The default coefficients are literature-informed initialization values. They are not universally calibrated diagnostic probabilities. They may not be transportable across:

- primary-care settings;
- specialty dizziness clinics;
- tertiary referral centers;
- emergency departments;
- different countries or health systems;
- different test protocols;
- different case definitions;
- different disease spectra.

## Synthetic-case disclaimer

The example cases are synthetic demonstrations. They are intended to show software behavior and network logic. They do not estimate diagnostic accuracy, calibration, safety, or clinical usefulness.

## Clinical validation required

Any clinical use would require, at minimum:

- locked model specification;
- appropriate IRB or ethics review where applicable;
- validation in representative clinical data;
- calibration assessment;
- error analysis;
- clinical governance;
- regulatory review if used for patient care;
- clear responsibility assignment for clinical decisions.
